# Scrum Framework Guide

## Three Pillars

### 1. Transparency
Work process visible to all.

### 2. Inspection
Frequent inspection of Scrum artifacts.

### 3. Adaptation
Adjust if deviation found.

## Three Roles

### Product Owner
- Manages Product Backlog
- Accepts/rejects Sprint Increment

### Scrum Master
- Ensures Scrum is understood
- Helps team self-organize

### Development Team
- Delivers product increment
- Self-organizing
